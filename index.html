About Me.

With nearly 5 years of experience in Supply Chain Management, I have honed my expertise in implementing innovative solutions that drive operational efficiency and enhance business outcomes. My journey has been one of continuous learning, enabling me to transform complex processes into actionable strategies that deliver measurable results.

As an Oracle Cloud and R12 E-Business Suite specialist, I’ve configured and implemented setups across modules like Project Manufacturing, Work in Process, Bill of Materials, and Inventory. My ability to translate business requirements into robust system configurations ensures seamless operations for stakeholders.

Proficiency in analytical tools, such as Excel, has allowed me to assess Supply Chain performance and identify opportunities for improvement. I’ve developed comprehensive reports and presentations that communicate analytical findings effectively, ensuring actionable recommendations for decision-makers.

Beyond technical expertise, I bring a strong focus on process improvement, risk management, and compliance, ensuring that supply chain and logistics operations remain efficient and resilient. My knowledge extends to designing data management systems and analytics strategies that enhance data quality and integration across sources.

When I’m not optimizing supply chain processes or enhancing Oracle configurations, I engage with business stakeholders to elicit and document requirements, mentor teams, and explore innovative methods to improve resource management. Let’s connect and discuss how we can drive value together!
